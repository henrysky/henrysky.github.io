Hi! I am Henry

I am an astronomy undergrad at the University of Toronto, you can contact me at henrysky.leung [at] mail.utoronto.ca

Learn About Me

I grew up in Hong Kong but moved to Toronto in 2014 to study university with a passion of studying astronomy and Canada is always my ideal place to live. Being an astronomer is always one of my dream since I was like 4. I am currently more interested in deep learning after I have studied a forth year physiology course on neurophysiology in 2017.


Currently, both astronomy and deep learning are my research interest. I am doing a reserach project on deep learning application in stellar and galactic astronomy. I am developing a python package called astroNN to facilitate my reserach. You can learn more about astroNN in Code section.
